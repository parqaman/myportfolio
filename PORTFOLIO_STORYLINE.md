This document contains a structured storyline and visual guide for your portfolio website.

## ⭐️ Visual Direction: Sophisticated Slate
A premium, calm, and professional aesthetic with a focus on editorial typography.

### Preferred Palette
- **Primary Background**: `#0B0F19` (Midnight Slate)
- **Secondary Background**: `#0F172A` (Deep Slate)
- **Accent High**: `#3B82F6` (Cyber Blue - for CTAs/Links)
- **Accent Low**: `#1E40AF` (Deep Blue - for subtle glows/gradients)
- **Primary Text**: `#F8FAFC` (Ghost White)
- **Secondary Text**: `#94A3B8` (Steel Silver)

### Visual Layout
- **Typography**: Paired serif/sans fallback. **Fraunces** for impactful headers and **Inter** for readability.
- **Sectioning**: Minimalist spacing with radial gradients (`bg-accent/5`) to provide depth.
